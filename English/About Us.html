About Us

Naíscoil na Seolta is an exciting new approach to pre-school education in East Belfast, offering an irish medium curriculum in an integrated setting.
The name of the pre-school (Sails Preschool) reflects our association with ship building in east Belfast, while simultaneously representing our children embarking on the beginning of their educational journey (Turas).
